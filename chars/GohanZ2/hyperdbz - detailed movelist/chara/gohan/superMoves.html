Masenko (LV1) (shocks)
Gohan's famous attack acts as a Denjin Hadoken; an energy projectile that electrifies and stuns the opponent on impact. Gohan can also charge up the projectile before fireing it, making it faster, stronger and improving it's shocking features. For zoning prowess, Gohan is able to launch the Masenko diagonally upwards. You can supercancel into the Masenko from his Ki Blasts. Finally, it has a limiter which prevents you from using it twice in a single combo.
When in Destiny Mode, the Masenko is changed to the Burst Masenko, which is a shorter ranged version that doesn't stun and is unchargeble, but comes out very fast, almost instantly.
Soaring Dragon Strike (LV1)
This Super move has Gohan dashing across the screen at high speed while being invurnerable to projectiles, and leaping up with an enormous uppercut as soon as he reaches the opponent, sending them flying high into the air.
Super Hurricane Kick (LV1)
Aerial spinning kick that hits multiple times. It's able to suck in the opponent if they're near and moreso if they're hit once, they're pulled in. Good combo-ender. You're able to supercancel into it during either of his Hurricane Kick special moves as well as his Diving Kick Flurry.
Masenko (LV2) (shocks)
Souped-up version, does more hits and damage. Note that this was changed from the previous build where it launched a spark instead of a projectile. Now it acts more like a buffed-up version of the Lv1.
When in Destiny Mode, the Masenko is changed to the Burst Masenko, which is a shorter ranged version that doesn't stun and is unchargeble, but comes out very fast, almost instantly. This Lvl2 version is really powerful!
Super Kamehameha (LV2)
Identical to Goku's Lv2 Super Kamehameha, except Gohan can fire it diagonally downwards from the air and lacks the option to fire it diagonally into the air while standing.
Soaring Dragon Strike (LV2)
Souped-up with a faster dash and more hits and damage! Also comes with a big aura surrounding his arm so you know Gohan means business!
Gekiretsu Ranbu (LV3)
Gohan's Ultimate is a counter move. If he's hit during his special pose, Gohan counters the opponent and destroys him with the badass beatdown combo he performed against Majin Buu. In Unleashed mode, Gekiretsu Ranbu becomes a standalone offensive move (no longer a counter) which can hit at mid-distance and you're able to combo into it, even able to catch aerial opponents with the shockwave it produces at the start. Also in Unleashed mode, Gohan gains access to different finishers for Gekiretsu Ranbu, which are performed automatically depending on the Unleashed Bar level;
Lv1 - Final Masenko.
Lv2 - Hi-Ex Blast Fist.
Lv3 - Bro Kamehameha.
