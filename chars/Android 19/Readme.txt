==================Super Motions==================

Explosion Wave:

D, B, F, y   (3000 power) <== if you hold "y" then attack will stronger

Special Combination:

D, B, F, x   (1000 power)

Super Blasts Attack:

D, B, F, b   (2000 power) <== if you hold "b" then attack will stronger

Purple Beam:

D, B, F, x   (1000 power)

==================Special Motions================

Kick Tp:

/F, b

Punch Up:

/F, x  or  /F, y

Absorb energy: 

/B, a   <== drain KI blasts , waves 

Fast Shoot:

D, F, z 

Absorb energy from opponent:

D, F, x  or D, F, y  <== push "x" then Android 19 drains life , push "y" then #19 drains power

KI blast:

c   <== if you add combination "D, F, c" then Android 19 use "Purple Beam"

Eye laser:

z   <== you can use it when you are crouch , stand on in air

==================Basic Motions==========================

Long Teletrans Forward:

/F, c

Long Teletrans Back:

/B, c

Short Teletrans Forward:

/F, z

Short Teletrans Back:

/B, z

Head Attack:

a or x  <== this work if Android 19 gets up. You must quicly press "a" or "x".
